🗑️ Delete Your FaithQuest Catholic Account

FaithQuest Catholic

We respect your right to delete your account and all associated data at any time. This page explains how to request account deletion.

What gets deleted

  • ✅ Your account (email address and authentication data)
  • ✅ All Grace Points, ranks, and badges
  • ✅ All activity logs (prayers, Mass attendance, Bible readings, etc.)
  • ✅ All journal entries
  • ✅ All parish and leaderboard data

What is NOT retained after deletion

We do not retain any personal data after account deletion. All data is permanently removed within 30 days of your request.

How to delete your account

Option 1 — Email us (recommended):
  1. Send an email to crusaderjoe22@gmail.com
  2. Use subject line: "Delete My FaithQuest Account"
  3. Include the email address linked to your account
  4. We will confirm deletion within 7 days

Option 2 — Delete directly in the app:
  1. Open FaithQuest Catholic
  2. Go to Player Profile (tap your avatar on the dashboard)
  3. Tap Sign Out → then contact us via email to complete full deletion
📧 Contact for deletion requests:
crusaderjoe22@gmail.com
We respond within 7 business days. Account data is fully deleted within 30 days.

Delete data only (keep account)

If you want to reset your spiritual progress data without deleting your account, email us at crusaderjoe22@gmail.com with the subject "Reset My FaithQuest Data". We will clear your activity data while keeping your account active.
