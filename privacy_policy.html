Privacy Policy

App Name: FaithQuest Catholic
Last Updated: March 29, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how FaithQuest Catholic ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information:

  • Email address – collected when you create an account via Google Firebase Authentication.
  • Spiritual activity data – prayers logged, Mass attendance, Bible readings, journal entries, Grace Points, and badges. This data is stored in your account to power the app's features.
  • Anonymous usage data – collected via Firebase Analytics to help us improve the app. This data cannot be used to identify you personally.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, phone number, or physical address.
  • We do not access your camera, contacts, or location.
  • We do not collect payment information (the app is free).
  • We do not show advertisements.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To save and display your spiritual progress within the app.
  • To send daily prayer reminder notifications (only if you enable them).
  • To improve the app using anonymous analytics data.

4. Journal Privacy

Journal entries are encrypted on your device using a PIN that only you know. We cannot read your journal entries.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Analytics, Messaging) – Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Bolls.life Bible API – used to fetch Bible text. No personal data is shared.
  • CatholicGallery.org – used to fetch daily Mass readings. No personal data is shared.

6. Data Security

Your data is stored securely on Google Firebase servers with industry-standard encryption in transit (TLS/HTTPS). Journal entries are additionally encrypted locally on your device.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at the email below. Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered.

8. Children's Privacy

FaithQuest Catholic is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.
